About the Band

The Los Altos High School Eagle Marching Band and Color Guard from Los Altos, California, directed by Ted Ferrucci, strives for excellence in every show it performs. The band visits Walden West for overnight band camp and competes in WBA competitions throughout the state of California.

In 2014, the Eagle Marching Band and Color Guard traveled to southern California to compete in Bands of America. While there, the band spent time in Disneyland where they attended a workshop and participated in a parade down the famous Main Street.
